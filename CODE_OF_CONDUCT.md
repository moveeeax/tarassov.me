# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, as its Code of Conduct.

The full text is published at:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: we want a friendly, harassment-free experience for everyone who takes
part in this project — contributors, maintainers, and users — regardless of
background or identity. Be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion
focused on the work.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces (issues, merge/pull requests,
discussions, commit messages) and when an individual is representing the project
in public spaces.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers at **conduct@tarassov.me**.
All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the
maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

For *security* vulnerabilities, do **not** open a public issue — follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards described
in the Contributor Covenant, and may take any action they deem appropriate, up
to and including a temporary or permanent ban, in response to behavior they
consider inappropriate. The Covenant's
[Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines)
are used to determine the consequences of any violation.
